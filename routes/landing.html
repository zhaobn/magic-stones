Once upon a time in the magic world ...

Some stones are magic stones, and some are normal stones.

A magic stone is indicated by having a border, like this:

And a normal stone does not have borders, like this:


For all the magic stones and normal stones:

  • their color can be red, yellow, or blue;
  • their shape can be circle, square, or diamond;
  • all stones are of the same size; and
  • magic stones can change the color and/or shape of normal stones.

As a newcomer to the magic world, you have the chance to watch how one specific magic stone changes a normal stone.

After that, you will be facing some other magic stones and normal stones. Give your best prediction at how the given magic stone changes the given normal stone.

You can consult the effects you saw by scolling up to the top of the page.

Hint: All the stones are in the same magic world and share the same magic rule.

Think carefully. Whenever you make a correct prediction, you earn a 10 cent bonus.

Comprehension questions

  3. You have access to the magic effects while making predictions.

  4. You earn a 10 cent bonus for each prediction you get right
