Once upon a time in a magic world ...

Some stones are magic stones, and some are normal stones.

A magic stone is indicated by having a border, like this:

And a normal stone does not have borders, like this:


For all the magic stones and normal stones:

  • their shape can be circle
    , square
    , or diamond
    ;
  • their color can be red, yellow, or blue;
  • stones differ only in their magic power, color, and shape;
  • magic stones can set the color and/or shape of normal stones into specific states.

As a newcomer to the magic world, you have the chance to watch one specific magic stone's effects on a normal stone.

After that, you will be facing some other magic stones and normal stones. Give your best prediction at the given magic stone's effects on the given normal stone.

You will make predictions for 15 pairs of magic stones and normal stones. Between each prediction case, you watch the original effects again as a reminder.

To help you make accurate predictions, a summary of the original effects you saw will be displayed at the top of the page.

Hint: All the stones are in the same magic world and share the same magic rule.

Think carefully. Your prediction is important for these stones.
